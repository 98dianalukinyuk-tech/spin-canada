Responsible participation

Poker is paid entertainment, not a guaranteed source of income. Decide limits before arrival, avoid chasing losses and pause when the experience stops feeling comfortable.

01

Set a time limit

Choose a leaving time before entering the room.

02

Set a spending limit

Use only money reserved for entertainment.

03

Take breaks

Step away from the table and reassess regularly.

04

Seek support

Provincial support services can provide confidential guidance.
